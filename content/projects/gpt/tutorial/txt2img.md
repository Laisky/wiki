## Models

text to image:

- openai dall-e-3
- sdxl-turbo (free)

image to image:

- sdxl-turbo (free)
- lcm (free)

{{< hint info >}}
Each image will take an average of 15 seconds to generate. Please be patient.

Free users can only generate 1 image per 10 minute.
{{</ hint >}}
